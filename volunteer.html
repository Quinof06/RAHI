Volunteer

Be a part of our group. Attend a meeting and decide where you can help RAHI. We always welcome ideas, research, and communication with the public about us. We welcome anyone in construction, land management, on a board or committee, or anyone that has any interest in aforementioned topics.

Leave A Legacy

There are so many ways to leave property to be used for affordable housing - either a buildable piece of land, vacant. By using and listing a dwelling to create two or more housing unites, aging in peace is preferred by our seniors and veterans, and providing assistance to them is our goal
